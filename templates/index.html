Welcome to the BOINC -> GRC Faucet

This faucet can help you get the first few GRC you need to set up Gridcoin for solo crunching (make a beacon). Help keep this faucet alive by sidestaking a portion of your staking proceeds to it.

Here's how to use it:
  1. Connect to one of our approved BOINC projects and start crunching work for them. If you are a new user, NFS@Home is strongly suggested as workunits validate very quickly. Otherwise, you may be waiting on another user to complete the same workunit which could take days!
  2. Your work will earn you credits. Once you have enough credits, paste the link to your user profile in the form below, along with your GRC address, to receive 2 GRC
  3. You must temporarily change your username/nickname at the project to your GRC address
  4. You may need to wait up to 24 hours after doing so, as we must wait for projects to publish your stats
  5. Some projects, such as World Community Grid, require you to enable stats exports in your user settings. See the Gridcoin Whitelist for which projects require this and how to do so.
  6. Existing BOINC users who maybe haven't crunched for a while: you must have at least some "recent average credit" ("RAC"), meaning you must have crunched at least one workunit in roughly the past 2 weeks. Your RAC can be very small, but it must be greater than 1

Faucet Rules

  • You can only use the faucet one time
  • If you already have a beacon setup, you can't use the faucet
  • If you have >2 GRC in your wallet, you can't use the faucet since you already have enough for a beacon

Donate

 This faucet is funded by people like you! You can donate directly to the faucet by sending GRC to {{FAUCETADDRESS}}.

You can also sidestake, which means donating a % of your staking rewards, this is a good way to donate small amounts over time. To add this to your wallet's sidestakes, go to hamburger menu (top left) --> help --> debug window --> console and type in the following two commands followed by enter. Replace the number 5 with the % you would like to donate from each stake.
  • enablesidestaking=1
  • sidestake={{ FAUCETADDRESS }},5
